About Lumex

Lumex Enterprise Solutions Pvt. Ltd. (Lumex) is an IT firm providing software and services in IBM technologies and outsourcing. Our depth of experience with delivering secure, end-to-end IBM technology solutions covers the full lifecycle. We do help our customers with software procurement, architecture advice, design and development through to deployment, administration, support and training.

The company is incorporated in October 2024 and our vision is to provide consistent delivery with a highly valued ability to execute. With our deep expertise in implementing solutions based on IBM technologies we aim to provide rapid ROI for your investment in IBM technology. We have our office located in Bangalore (India)
